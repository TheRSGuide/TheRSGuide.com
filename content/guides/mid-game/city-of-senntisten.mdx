---
title: City of Senntisten
description: Unlock powerful Ancient Magicks combat spells
---

The City of Senntisten quest unlocks four Ancient Magicks spells: Smoke Cloud, Animate Dead, Exsanguinate, and Incite Fear. These are all very useful spells that can aid you at all levels of combat.

**Smoke Cloud**: A debuff that increases your **critical strike damage** for all combat styles.

**Exsanguinate**: An autocast spell for magic, providing increased damage to basic abilities as well as enhancing the **Wrack** ability.

**Incite Fear**: An autocast spell for magic providing an adrenaline reduction to cast the **Tsunami** ability.

**Animate Dead**: An essential **aspect**, used while wearing magic tank gear that greatly reduces the damage taken.
