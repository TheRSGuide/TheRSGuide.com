In the mid game, we'll focus on quests that will set your character up for success in the late game. This includes important unlocks like **Ancient Curses**, **Prifddinas**, and the **Invention** skill. We will also begin time-locked content like **Player Owned Ports** so you can jump start your progression!

## Key Objectives

- Unlock Ancient Curses for powerful combat prayers
- Complete Plague's End to access Prifddinas
- Unlock the Invention skill at level 80 in Divination, Crafting, and Smithing
- Start Player Owned Ports for long-term rewards
- Obtain the Slayer Helmet for combat bonuses
