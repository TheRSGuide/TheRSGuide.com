---
title: Slayer Helmet
description: Massive damage and accuracy boost for Slayer tasks
---

Slayer helmet provides a large boost to damage and accuracy when on your active slayer task. Makes slayer a viable training method and has endgame PvM applications as some bosses count as respective tasks.

## Components Required

To create the full Slayer Helmet, you'll need to obtain these drops:

1. **Black mask** - Cave Horrors (level 58 Slayer)
2. **Hexcrest** - Jungle Strykewyrms (level 73 Slayer, off-task)
3. **Focus Sight** - Desert Strykewyrms (level 77 Slayer, off-task)
4. **Spectral Lens** - Profane Scabarites (level 87 Slayer)

## Quest Requirements

- Stolen Hearts
- Diamond in the Rough
- Gertrude's Cat
- Icthlarin's Little Helper
- The Restless Ghost
- Smoking Kills

## Skill Requirements

| Skill | Level |
|-------|-------|
| Slayer | 87 |
