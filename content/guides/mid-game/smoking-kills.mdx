---
title: Smoking Kills
description: Unlock full Slayer point rewards
---


Completing **Smoking Kills** is essential for efficient Slayer training. Before finishing this quest, you only receive 50% of the possible Slayer points from tasks. After completion, you will earn the full amount of Slayer points for each task, making it much easier to unlock rewards, extend tasks, and skip undesired assignments. This quest is a key milestone for progressing in Slayer and maximizing your point gains.
