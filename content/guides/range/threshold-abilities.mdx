---
title: Ranged Threshold Abilities
description: Powerful abilities that cost 15% adrenaline
---

Just like in the basic abilities section, **Ranged** and **Magic** tend to share a lot of *similar feeling* thresholds.

As covered in [Combat Basics](/getting-started/combat-basics), all of these thresholds will require **50%** adrenaline to cast, and will cost **15%** adrenaline.

---

## 'Simple' Thresholds

### ![Snap Shot](https://runescape.wiki/images/Snap_Shot.png?5a703) Snap Shot

Snap Shot deals **2** hits dealing **120%** and **165%** average damage each, for a total of **285%** with a **20.4s** cooldown.

---

### ![Shadow Tendrils](https://runescape.wiki/images/Shadow_Tendrils.png?1b3ac) Shadow Tendrils

> This ability is obtained via a codex after [The Dig Site](/guides/early-game/dig-site) quest.

Shadow Tendrils deals a single hit of **235%** average damage, and a self hit to you of **117.5%** damage. This ability is guaranteed to **Critical Strike** resulting in a **1.5x** damage buff at level 90, leading to a damage value of **352.5%**.

---

## Channelled Abilities

All abilities in this section are Channelled Abilities. This means they must be cast for longer than a **Global Cooldown (1.8s)** to deal their full damage. Remember to watch your channel bar mentioned in the [Buff Bar](/setup/buff-bar) section.

### ![Rapid Fire](https://runescape.wiki/images/Rapid_Fire.png?dd564) Rapid Fire

Rapid Fire deals **8** hits, with one every **0.6s**. Each hit deals **70%** average damage, for a total damage of **560%** over **4.8s**.

---

## Area of Effect

### ![Bombardment](https://runescape.wiki/images/Bombardment.png?24372) Bombardment

Bombardment deals **140%** average damage to your target, and up to **9** enemies within **1 tile** of your target.

---

## Stuns

### ![Tight Bindings](https://runescape.wiki/images/Tight_Bindings.png?00b83) Tight Bindings

Tight Bindings is a low damaging threshold, with a **15s** cooldown. When cast this deals **130%** average damage, **stunning** your target for **3.6s** and **binding** your target for **9.6s**.
