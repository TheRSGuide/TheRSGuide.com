Here we're going to cover 3 of the Ranged Ultimate Abilities.

As covered in [Combat Basics](/getting-started/combat-basics), all of these Ultimates will cost **100%** adrenaline to cast unless stated otherwise.

---

### ![Death's Swiftness](https://runescape.wiki/images/Death%27s_Swiftness.png?1f01c) Death's Swiftness

Death's Swiftness is an Ultimate that is cast around your location, empowering you for **30.6s** with a **60s** cooldown. During this time, when standing inside your Death's Swiftness, all Ranged attacks are increased by **50%**.

Note that **bleed** abilities **do not** benefit from this buff.

> Can be upgraded to ![Greater Death's Swiftness](https://runescape.wiki/images/Greater_Death%27s_Swiftness.png?87c14) **Greater Death's Swiftness** via an ability codex - increases duration from **30.6s** to **37.8s**.

---

### ![Incendiary Shot](https://runescape.wiki/images/Incendiary_Shot.png?5b8d4) Incendiary Shot

When cast, Incendiary Shot will fire an explosion at your target, detonating **3s** later onto your target and up to **9** enemies within **1 tile** of your target, for **300%** average damage per target. You will also gain **8%** adrenaline from every **Critical Strike** for the next **30s**. Critical Strikes generated from the initial cast will generate adrenaline. This ability has a **60s** cooldown.

---

### ![Deadshot](https://runescape.wiki/images/Deadshot.png?a3efe) Deadshot

Deadshot is a damage over time ability. When cast, this will deal an initial hit of **125%** average damage, with **5** more hits of **70%** over the next **6s**, for a total damage of **475%**. This ability has a **30s** cooldown.

> This ability can be upgraded by obtaining the [Igneous Kal-Xil](/guides/late-game/zuk-cape), reducing the adrenaline cost to **60%**. This will also increase the damage of the primary hit from **125%** to **135%**, and will increase the additional number of hits from **5** to **7**, for a total average damage of **625%**.
