---
title: The Dig Site
description: Unlock powerful Tendrils abilities and progress towards Ancient Curses
---

Completing The Dig Site quest provides a solid amount of early Mining experience which will give you a head start in the skill.

In addition to the experience, finishing the quest unlocks three powerful abilities regularly used in combat: **Shadow Tendrils**, **Blood Tendrils**, and **Smoke Tendrils**. You'll also gain access to **Ice Asylum**, a healing ability for tougher encounters. Furthermore, The Dig Site is one of many prerequisites for unlocking **Ancient Curses**, a set of advanced prayers that significantly increase your combat capabilities later in the game.

Overall, this quest is a crucial early milestone for both skill progression and unlocking important combat tools.
