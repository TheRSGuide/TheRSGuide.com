In the early game, we'll focus on quality of life unlocks, ability unlocks and important quests with rewards that benefit your progression. This includes unlocking the **Ancient** and **Lunar spellbooks**, as well as starting the **Penguin quest series** and unlocking the **Dive** ability.

## Key Objectives

- Unlock important lodestones for fast travel
- Complete quests that provide significant XP rewards
- Unlock essential abilities like Devotion, Sacrifice, and Transfigure
- Start working towards the Ancient and Lunar spellbooks
- Begin the Penguin quest series
