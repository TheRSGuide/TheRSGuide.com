**Overloads** are essential potions for endgame PvM and should be used at all times in combat. They provide a massive stat boost and reapply the boost every 15 seconds, allowing for the use of Saradomin Brews in combat.

> Requires 90 Herblore & Evil Dave's Big Day Out quest (for 5 level boost) **OR** 96 Herblore
