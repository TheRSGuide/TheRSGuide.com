In the late game, we'll be focusing on the most critical unlocks in RuneScape. This includes completing the **Extinction** quest for the **Ring of Vigour passive**, unlocking **Amascut, the Devourer** boss fight, creating **Overloads**, and getting your first **Zuk cape**!

## Key Objectives

- Complete Extinction for the Ring of Vigour passive effect
- Create Elder Overloads for maximum stat boosts
- Obtain a Zuk cape from the TzKal-Zuk fight
- Unlock access to endgame bosses
- Complete important quest chains for passives and abilities
