---
title: Extinction
description: Unlock Ring of Vigour passive and major endgame content
---

Completing **Extinction** provides several major rewards that are highly valuable for late-game progression:

- **700,000 experience** in lamps for fast training (requires level 75 in chosen skill).
- **Permanent Ring of Vigour effect:** Always save 10% adrenaline on ultimates, no ring needed.
- **Effigy Incubator access:** Unlocks the best Runecrafting XP and unique rewards.
- **Enriched Pontifex Ring:** Upgrade your Pontifex Ring for extra benefits in Elder God Wars Dungeon.

Overall, Extinction is a key quest for unlocking powerful account upgrades and essential late-game content.
