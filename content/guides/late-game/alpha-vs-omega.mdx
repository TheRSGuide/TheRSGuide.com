---

Completing **Alpha vs Omega** unlocks access to **Rasial, the First Necromancer**, one of the most challenging endgame bosses in RuneScape. Rasial is the exclusive source of **First Necromancer** equipment, a seven-piece best-in-slot (BiS) Necromancy gear set that dramatically boosts your power in the Necromancy combat style.
