## Recommended for New Players

If you're new to RuneScape combat, we recommend starting with **Necromancy**. It's designed specifically for new players with:
- Built-in tutorials
- Powerful early game abilities
- Self-sustaining conjures that heal and protect you

Once you're comfortable with combat basics, you can branch out to explore other combat styles.
