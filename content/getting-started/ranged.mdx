---
title: Ranged Combat
description: Overview of Ranged combat style in RuneScape
---

Ranged is a feast or famine combat style with limited early game power but significant late game upgrades that result in the most damage output of any combat style. Buyer beware though, as executing high level ranged rotations is a complicated endeavour.

In addition, Ranged underperforms compared to all other styles in the early and mid game.

## Combat Style Analysis

| Metric | Rating |
|--------|--------|
| Ease of Use | 3/10 |
| Early Game Power | 3/10 |
| Late Game Power | 9/10 |

## Key Strengths

- **Highest late game damage** - With proper gear and rotations, Ranged deals the most damage
- **Safe distance** - Can attack from range, avoiding some mechanics
- **Strong special attacks** - Many powerful ranged weapons have useful special attacks
- **Excellent upgrades** - Late game gear significantly boosts performance

## Considerations

- Rotations are complex and require practice
- Early game is weaker compared to other styles
- Ammunition costs can add up

## Getting Started

To begin training Ranged, you'll need:
- A ranged weapon (bow, crossbow, or throwing weapons)
- Ammunition (arrows, bolts, or thrown items)
- Ranged armor appropriate to your level
